**What is it to be a Bat?** In 1974, T. Nagel offered a transcendental point about mind-body problem and subjective experience. The way I feel, my emotions as well as the senses I'm activating to perceive the world, are unique. Then how do we know that everyone is feeling the same way?

I am deeply fascinating by how `Evolution has shaped animal's incredible perceptive abilities`. Cognition, the process of informations, is the field I would like to explore, in order to apply concepts for conservation purposes.
Right now, I am studying `invertebrate cognition` at Tours, especially the role of pollutants found in water on learning and memory abilities in aquatic insects.
By understanding how animals perceive the world, by putting ourselves into their place, I strongly believe that we'll be able to get inspiration to develop applications as well as acting in conservation and preserve their environment.
